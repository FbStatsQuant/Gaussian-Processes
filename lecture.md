# Lecture Notes on Gaussian Processes

## Introduction
Gaussian Processes (GPs) are a powerful tool for regression and classification tasks. They define a distribution over functions and can provide a measure of uncertainty in predictions.

## Key Concepts
- **Mean function**: Represents the expected output of the process.
- **Covariance function (Kernel)**: Encodes the relationship between points in the input space.

## Applications
- Used in machine learning for tasks like regression and classification.
- Effective in situations where models are uncertain or complex.

## Conclusion
Gaussian Processes offer a probabilistic approach to modeling functions, making them suitable for various applications.
